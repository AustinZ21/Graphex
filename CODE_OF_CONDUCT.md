# CGA Code Of Conduct

CGA welcomes contributors, users, and maintainers who want to build useful open
source developer tooling together. Participation in this project should be
professional, respectful, and focused on the work.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful of differing viewpoints and experience levels.
- Give and receive technical feedback with care.
- Keep discussions focused on the project and its users.
- Credit others for their ideas, reports, and contributions.
- Report security issues through the security process rather than public issue
  threads.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, intimidation, or sustained personal attacks.
- Discriminatory language or conduct.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Knowingly submitting code, documentation, or assets you do not have the right
  to contribute.
- Disrupting project spaces after maintainers ask you to stop.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, block participation, or take
other reasonable action to protect the project community. Enforcement decisions
should be proportionate, documented when practical, and focused on preserving a
safe and productive project space.
